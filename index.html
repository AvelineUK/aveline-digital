Local SEO

GBP management focuses on your Google Business Profile — the listing that appears in Maps and the Local Pack. Local SEO is broader: it covers your website's organic rankings, how your business is listed across the web (citations), and making sure everything is consistent and optimised to help you show up in local searches. The two services complement each other well, but each delivers value on its own.

Citations are mentions of your business name, address, and phone number on other websites — directories like Yell, Yelp, Thomson Local, and dozens of others. When these are consistent and accurate, they act as a trust signal to Google and help strengthen your local rankings. When they're inconsistent — old addresses, wrong phone numbers — they can quietly hold you back.

Local SEO is a longer-term investment than GBP management. Citation cleanup and consistency improvements can take 4-12 weeks to be reflected in search. Keyword ranking improvements typically build over 3–6 months as Google indexes changes to your site and picks up the updated signals across the web. We'll set realistic expectations from the start and show you progress in your monthly report.
